About Dial-up

Dial-up internet was the gateway to the online world for millions of people in the 1990s and early 2000s. It was characterized by its distinctive connection sounds and slow speeds, but it opened up a world of possibilities.

Despite its limitations, dial-up internet paved the way for the high-speed connections we enjoy today and played a crucial role in the development of the modern internet.
